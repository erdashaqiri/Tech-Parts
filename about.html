About TechParts

Welcome to TechParts, your premier destination for high-quality computer components in North Macedonia. Founded in 2015, we've grown from a small local shop to the leading online retailer of PC hardware in the region.

At TechParts, we're passionate about technology and committed to helping our customers build their dream PCs. Whether you're a hardcore gamer, a creative professional, or just looking to upgrade your home computer, we have the components you need at competitive prices.

Our team consists of experienced tech enthusiasts who are always ready to provide expert advice and support. We carefully select all our products to ensure they meet the highest standards of quality and performance.

Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Maestro), bank transfers, and cash on delivery for orders within Skopje. We're also working on adding PayPal support in the near future.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 14-day return policy for all unused products in their original packaging. For defective items, we provide a 2-year warranty according to Macedonian consumer protection laws. Please contact our support team to initiate a return.

Do you offer assembly services?

Yes! For an additional fee, our technicians can assemble your PC using the components you've purchased from us. We also offer system testing and software installation services. Contact us for more details and pricing.

How long does delivery take?

For Skopje, we offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 2 PM. For other cities in North Macedonia, delivery typically takes 1-2 business days. We also provide express delivery options for urgent orders.

Can I pick up my order from your store?

Absolutely! We encourage customers to visit our showroom in Centar Skopje to pick up their orders and see our products in person. Our address is Bul. Partizanski Odredi 15, and we're open Monday to Saturday from 9 AM to 7 PM.
